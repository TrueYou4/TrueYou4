Because in a world of fakes, realness matters more than ever.

Wave uses verification technology to create the world's first truly authentic social platform, where every moment is verifiable and every experience is real.

Be Yourself

Wave is the home of unfiltered moments. Share life as it happens — not staged, not altered, just real.

Privacy by Design

Privacy controls let you choose exact location sharing and selective visibility. Your data stays encrypted, and you control exactly who sees what, when.

Photo Verification

Advanced EXIF forensics and C2PA content credentials ensure every photo is authentic. Our software detects manipulation, verifies timestamps, and confirms location metadata matches your check-in.
